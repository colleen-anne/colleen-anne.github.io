About Me

I've always believed that is important to try and experience anything and everything. When I'm not reading a good book (or neurology paper), you can probably find me spending time with friends, whether it's at a concert or exploring nature. My outdoor hobbies include gardening, bouldering, hiking in the mountains, skiing, and biking everywhere that I can. Painting, making music, and writing are other activities I enjoy in my spare time. I welcome open discussions, new concepts, and things that take me out of my comfort zone; like Voltaire said, "if we do not find pleasant things we will at least find new things".

My ultimate goal is to complete my undergraduate degree with a minor in Art History and proceed to get my Bachelor of Education. I dream of being able to share my passion for learning by teaching Computer Science and Art at the highschool level.
